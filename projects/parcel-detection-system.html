Tamper Detection System

Advanced computer vision model for automated parcel detection and damage assessment - identifies parcels and classifies them as good or damaged

Status: Completed
Duration: Sep 2023 - Nov 2023
Technologies: YOLOv8, Computer Vision, AI

Project Overview

The Parcel Detection System uses advanced computer vision technology to automatically detect parcels and assess their condition in logistics and delivery environments. This comprehensive system identifies parcels and classifies them as either good or damaged, enhancing operational efficiency and quality control.

Project Guidance & Supervision

This project was developed under the expert guidance of Prof. M.V. Narayana Sir, Head of Department (ECE) and R&D Dean at KSRM College of Engineering. His mentorship in computer vision applications and logistics automation provided the foundation for this comprehensive parcel detection and damage assessment system.

Key Features

Parcel Identification

Accurate detection of parcels in various environments with high precision and reliability.

Damage Assessment

Classification of parcels as good or damaged for quality control and logistics optimization.

Quality Assessment

Evaluation of parcel condition and integrity to ensure delivery standards.

Real-time Processing

Instant detection and classification results for seamless logistics operations.

Technical Implementation

The system utilizes YOLOv8 object detection model optimized for parcel detection scenarios. The model was trained on a comprehensive dataset of parcel images covering various sizes, shapes, and environmental conditions.

Model: YOLOv8 Custom Trained
Accuracy: 92% Detection Rate
Processing: Real-time Performance
Integration: API-based Deployment
